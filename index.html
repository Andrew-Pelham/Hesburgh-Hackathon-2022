Mission

At the end of every academic year, students rid themselves of old furniture and broken appliances. At Notre Dame, the Goodwill of Southbend makes their rounds to pickup unwanted items to stock at their franchises. However, there is a gap between what students are throwing out and what Goodwill can take. E-Waste begins to pile up as busted electronics and unaccepted items get binned with other trash; many of these parts are still usable, and with the right care, could be brought back to life.

part::out fills the gap found in the move-out process. Students can log items, and with groups like CS4Good, mobilize to build and repair fully functional PC setups for the greater Michana area.
